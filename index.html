About me

From a young age, I've always been passionate about drawing. It brings me constant joy and allows me to express my emotions and creativity. My work often brings to life various characters, their emotions, as well as worlds of fantasy, fairy tales, and comics. Studying art has given me the opportunity to understand what I truly excel at. I discovered that, in addition to character design, I am also fascinated by simple and effective visual communication, such as user interfaces (UI) and minimalist forms.
